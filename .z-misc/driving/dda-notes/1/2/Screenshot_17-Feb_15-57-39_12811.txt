6.2 Effect of narcotic drugs on drivers while driving:

Medicines and narcotic drugs taken by the driver without
supervision of the doctor affect ability of the driver to
driver in an appropriate way. It could have negative
effect on concentration, mood, the ability to take
decisions and the reactions while driving

So, be careful not to take medicines with label that warns
to avoid driving when taken, and to read medicine
prescription or seek doctor consultation about the effect
lof the medicine on driving.

Medicines that have effect on the driver while driving:

/ Some medicines for the treatment of blood pressure,
nausea, allergies, inflammations and fungal infections

}  Tranquilizers, painkillers and sleeping pills.

L Some Diet pills

L Some cold and flu medicines
